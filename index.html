Why do many images show a car?

  • It's an artifact of the way SDXL was trained. It appears consistently, but not always, for any prompt doesn't include a specific subject. Surprisingly, the car appears for many artists, for many different seeds, and for many different prompts and negative prompts. For example, I use the non-specific ğŸ¨ prompt, "artwork image". I picked that intentionally to reveal the subject matter biases SDXL has for each artist. This artifact of SDXL probably reveals a bias of the model towards generating car images.
